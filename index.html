What is an open bite?

If your upper and lower teeth don't contact with each other when your mouth is closed.

What is an overbite?

It is also called buck teeth. An overbite is when your upper front teeth protrude ( stick out) beyond your lower front teeth Most people have at least a little overbite.

What is an underbite?

An underbite when you close you close your mouth and your lower front teeth are in front of your upper teeth in many cases, this is actually a problem with your lower jaw being too forward

What are Gap teeth?

When you have extra space b/w two or more of your teeth is called as Gap teeth.

What is crossbite?

It is a bite problem where the upper teeth and lower teeth don't come together or bite in the correct position.

It is caused by either tooth position, jaw position or a combination of both.

What is crowding?

Crowding is the lack of space for all the teeth to fit normally within the jaws. Crowding occurs when there is disharmony in the tooth to jaw size relationship, or when the teeth are larger than the available space.
